The Path to Engineering Schools

In France, the pathway to top engineering schools (Grandes Écoles) is a complex journey through high school specializations, preparatory classes (CPGE), and competitive entrance exams. But not everyone starts from the same line.

Gender, wealth, and geography create invisible barriers that filter out talent long before the exam room. This interactive documentary maps those barriers with real data.

The Road Ahead

These data tell a clear story: access to France's engineering elite is shaped by factors far beyond academic merit. From the specializations students choose in high school, to the filtering of the Parcoursup platform, to the geographic lottery of where CPGE programs exist — the obstacles compound.

EquiLearn believes that talent is equally distributed; opportunity is not. By making these patterns visible, we take the first step toward equity.
